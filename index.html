Crochet: a fiber art

Crochet is a textile craft that creates fabric by interlocking loops of yarn or thread using a single hook. Derived from the French word for "small hook", crochet is distinct from knitting because it uses only one tool, a crochet hook, and completes one stitch at a time

Tools required for crochet:

  1. Crochet hook
  2. Ball of yarn
  3. Scissors
  4. Stitch markers
  5. Needle
  6. Measuring tape

There are many free tutorials and courses available on the internet to get you started on your crochet journey. Whether you are a beginner or pro, youtube videos are a good resource for learning more about crochet. From stitches to whole projects. Give one a try today!
